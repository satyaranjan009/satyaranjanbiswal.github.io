Research

I specialize in computational biophysics, focusing on proton and ion transport mechanisms in hydrated biological systems using Molecular Dynamics (MD), Quantum Mechanics/Molecular Mechanics (QM/MM), and machine learning techniques.

Technical Skills

  • Computational Chemistry: GROMACS, AMBER, NAMD, Gaussian, CP2K, ORCA, VASP, DFTB+.
  • Programming: Python, C, C++, Fortran, Tcl, Shell scripting.
  • Machine Learning: Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Keras, XGBoost.
  • Visualization Tools: VMD, Chimera, ChimeraX, PyMol.
  • High-Performance Computing: HPC cluster simulations.
